Premium Concentrated Bird's Nest with Rock Sugar

1 bottle x 160g

TruLife Concentrated Bird's Nest with Rock Sugar is a premium, completely natural food prepared with only the best grade of 100% authentic pure bird's nest and rock sugar. Containing a richer texture and more ingredients, it contains no artificial preservatives, flavouring or colouring, retaining the nourishing benefits and natural goodness of our traditional best-tasting recipe.
